Biography

I am an Associate Professor of Operations Research in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics at HKUST. As a scholar, I am committed to developing general methodologies for data-driven decision-making. I take advantage of the growing availability of data and computing power, integrating tools from simulation, stochastics, and statistics to solve real-world challenges.

My research focuses on methodological advances in stochastic simulation and optimization, decision analytics, and causal machine learning, with applications in service operations management, digital economy, and financial technology. My work has been published in top-tier journals such as Management Science, Operations Research, and Mathematics of Operations Research. I also serve as an Associate Editor for Management Science.
