On the Dynamics of a Finite Buffer Queue Conditioned on the Amount of Loss

Xiaowei Zhang, Peter W. Glynn
December 2010
DOI

Abstract

This paper is concerned with computing large deviations asymptotics for the loss process in a stylized queueing model that is fed by a Brownian input process. In addition, the dynamics of the queue, conditional on such a large deviation in the loss, is calculated. Finally, the paper computes the quasi-stationary distribution of the system and the corresponding dynamics, conditional on no loss occurring.
